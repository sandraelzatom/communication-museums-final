# Visual Research: The Synesthesia of Signaling

## I. Chromatic Evolution
This project uses color as a chronological marker:
* **Bronze & Ochre:** Represents the era of physical mechanics and copper wiring.
* **Electric Indigo:** Represents the transition into the vacuum tube and radio era.
* **Neural Cyan:** Represents the weightless, high-speed flow of digital data.

## II. Motion Research
We implemented **AOS (Animate On Scroll)** to simulate the "Signal Velocity." As the user scrolls, the content arrives from the periphery—mimicking the arrival of a data packet at its destination.

## III. The "Stunning" Factor
To ensure 100% impact, the "Deep Dive" utilizes moving radial gradients. This represents the fluid nature of communication waves, moving from rigid wires to invisible atmosphere.
