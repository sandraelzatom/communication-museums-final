# Research & Orchestration Log: Museum of Human Connectivity
**Project:** Final Assignment - AI-Orchestrated Museum Site
**Date:** May 8, 2026

---

## 1. Core Narrative Strategy
The goal of this informational product is to guide the visitor through the **Evolution of Information Velocity**. We move from physical, localized signals (Smoke/Drums) to global, instantaneous neural networks (AI).

### Subject Milestones:
* **The Primal Pulse (40,000 BCE):** Research into the use of rhythmic patterns and visual signals as the first "binary" systems.
* **The Gutenberg Shift (1440 CE):** Analysis of the transition from oral/scribal culture to mass-produced literacy.
* **The Electronic Age (1830s):** The telegraph as the "Victorian Internet"—the first time information moved faster than a horse.
* **The Digital Mind (21st Century):** The current era where communication is synthesized by Large Language Models (LLMs).

## 2. Design Logic (The Three Pillars)
I applied the required framework to ensure the site is intentional and professional:

| Pillar | Selection | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Design Style** | Swiss Modernism | Uses a strict grid and bold sans-serif type to make complex history easily scannable. |
| **Brand Archetype** | The Sage | Establishes the museum as a source of wisdom and truth. Tone is objective and enlightened. |
| **Cialdini Principle** | Authority | I used historical evidence, expert quotes, and academic timelines to create trust. |

---

## 3. AI Orchestration Workflow
This project was built using a structured AI-human collaboration:

1.  **Phase 1: Synthesis:** Used Gemini to cross-reference historical dates and compress 40,000 years of history into 6 digestible "Eras."
2.  **Phase 2: Visual Direction:** Prompted AI to generate a Swiss-style architectural lobby (`hero.jpg`) that reflects the "Echoes to Electrons" theme.
3.  **Phase 3: Code Refinement:** I acted as the Lead Developer, directing the AI to implement **Tailwind CSS** for styling and **AOS.js** for the scroll-based tour experience.
4.  **Phase 4: Critique & Iteration:** I used AI to review the accessibility and visual hierarchy, leading to the addition of high-contrast "Era Tags" for better readability.
